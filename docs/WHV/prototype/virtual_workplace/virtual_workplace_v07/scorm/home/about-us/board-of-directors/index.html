Board of directors

The GCBH board represents the diversity of our community and provides strategic and financial advice to ensure all decisions and activities meet the mission, vision, and values of GCBH.

Kevin Walsh
Accountant
Kevin is an experienced Certified Practicing Accountant (CPA) who brings strong financial acumen and understanding of fiscal responsibility to the board.
Juan Russo
Marketing and Communications Executive
As an experienced and qualified marketing and communications executive Juan provides valuable insight and support to the strong positioning of GCBH in the community.
Gabriel Cohen
Not-for-profit CEO
Gabriel’s brings years of executive experience working in the not-for-profit sector, and with that has a strong understanding of the sustainability challenges facing the sector.
Sara Li
Community Engagement Manager
As an active community leader within the migrant and refugee communities Sara brings to the board a strong understanding of the opportunities that these communities can offer to the Wiagara region.
Ryan Law
Disability Services Manager
As an experienced Disability Services Manager Ryan brings to the board extensive experience in quality compliance and audit management of funded working environments.
David Bhatt
Religious leader
As a respected religious leader in the Waigara Region David supports the board through his understanding of the intersectional complexity represented across the region.
Zoë Katsaros
Recreation and Sports Manager
Zoe brings to the board her experience as a Recreation and Sports Manager and in particular her involvement and experience in supporting the changing position of sport for women.
Adi Grover
Policy Manager
A well-known and respected face in Local Government Adi brings to the board an understanding of government policy and practice learnt through years in government administration.
