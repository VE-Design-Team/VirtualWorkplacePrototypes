Gentown Arts Centre

The Gentown Arts Centre supports a vibrant, innovative and active arts community. Students in the GCBH Arts Programs sell their art work at the centre for a 5% commission.

Women’s Health Victoria is a state-wide women’s health promotion, advocacy and support service that have provided gender equity training for GCBH staff and stakeholders.
