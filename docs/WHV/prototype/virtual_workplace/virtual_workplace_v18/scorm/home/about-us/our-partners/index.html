Our partners

Select each of the headings to learn more about our partners. When you have finished, select again to close the section.

GCCI is the local representative body for owners, entrepreneurs, and stakeholders to have their say on the future direction of business in Gentown. They are committed to helping small business start-ups and support the Gentown Community & Business Hub achieve this outcome. GCCI have representation at the Business & Tourism Regional Alliance (BTRA) and provide input to regional planning and events on behalf of Chamber members.

BTRA represents Hospitality and Tourism Associations and Chamber of Commerce members in the Wiagara Region, who in turn represent their local business and tourism community.

Gentown Business Owners Network is a local group of service-minded business owners in the Gentown community, who through their work and fundraising activities support new business start-ups and community clubs. From supporting the leaders of tomorrow through their youth recognition program to providing grants for sports teams the network members are making a huge difference in the Gentown community.

The Gentown Arts Centre supports a vibrant, innovative and active community.

Home to more than 20 creative artists, the artists provide encouragement to new and emerging artists including the GCBH Arts program participants, sharing their skills and knowledge and collaborating with aspiring artists to raise the profile of creative arts in the community

RWBN recognises and values the ongoing contributions of women to the business community of the greater Wiagara Region.

Running the Women’s Leadership Gala and Women in Business Award to celebrate women leaders in the region, RWBN hosts a series of events, including panel discussions with business experts, that bring to light the various work issues affecting women and men across all sectors.

The network also plays an active role in the local sporting communities by providing opportunities for girls and women to participate in sport.
