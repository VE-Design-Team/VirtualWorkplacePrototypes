Organisation chart

This organisation chart illustrates the reporting relationships between staff at GCBH. Our Chief Executive Officer (CEO) leads a passionate leadership team, the six roles highlighted in blue report directly to the CEO. These include the Executive Assistant, Business Services Manager, Employment and Training Manager, Community Programs Manager, Finance and Administration Manager and Human Resources Manager.
