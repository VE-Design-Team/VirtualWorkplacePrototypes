Organisational overview

The Gentown Community & Business Hub (GCBH) is a community driven space. We provide a wide range of services to support small business development, education, employment, recreation and social connection in the Gentown community.

Our Business Hub encourages community growth by helping local businesses and entrepreneurs thrive. Located on Main St. in Gentown, the hub offers office space, workshops, reception services, a high-speed fibre internet service and a supportive environment.

Businesses should serve the community and we are interested in strengthening both. Our programs and services are for the young and old, men and women, and people of all backgrounds. We look for businesses that align with our vision and mission to foster opportunities for the people of Gentown to flourish and succeed, whatever their circumstances.

Funded in part by the State and Commonwealth Governments, we provide low cost services for Health Care and Pension Card Holders.
