Policies & procedures

There are a number of policies and procedures that you need be aware of in the workplace. These are important for guiding decisions and actions in the organisation and helping employees understand what behaviours are most appropriate.

GCBH Policies have been adapted from the pool of policies found in the Institute of Community Directors Australia, Policy Bank. https://www.communitydirectors.com.au/icda/policybank/
